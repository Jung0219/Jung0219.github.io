OMR Sheet Grading System

Summary:

Developed a fully automated Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) grading system using OpenCV and NumPy to detect and score answer sheets from scanned images. The program applies advanced image preprocessing, perspective correction, and contour detection to accurately identify answer bubbles. It distinguishes filled vs. unfilled responses using pixel intensity thresholds and compares detected answers against a predefined answer key to compute scores automatically.

Key Features:

  • Implemented contour sorting and shape analysis to locate and label circular answer bubbles.
  • Performed perspective transformation for alignment correction of scanned sheets.
  • Applied morphological operations for noise removal and hole filling.
  • Automatically graded 20-question multiple-choice sheets with 100% accuracy under varied lighting and skew conditions.
  • Visualized results by overlaying correct answers in green and incorrect ones in red, along with the total percentage score.
